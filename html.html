Spaghetti
Carbonara
⭐ 4.9 · 2.3k saves · Italian Classic
30
Minutes
2
Servings
620
Calories
🛒 Ingredients
  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 100g Guanciale or Pancetta
  • 3 large Egg Yolks
  • 60g Pecorino Romano, grated
  • Freshly ground Black Pepper
  • Salt (for pasta water)
👨‍🍳 Steps
  1. 1

    Boil salted water and cook spaghetti until al dente, reserving 1 cup of pasta water.

  2. 2

    Fry guanciale in a pan over medium heat until crispy. Remove from heat.

  3. 3

    Whisk egg yolks with pecorino and a generous amount of black pepper.

  4. 4

    Toss hot pasta in the pan with guanciale. Off the heat, stir in the egg mixture, adding pasta water to get a silky sauce.
